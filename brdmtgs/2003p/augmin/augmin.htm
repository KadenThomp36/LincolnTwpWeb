LINCOLN TOWNSHIP PLANNING COMMISSION MEETING MINUTES

 

August 5, 2003

Joseph Leigeb – Chairperson, Sheila Mesler – Vice-Chairperson

Members: Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza

 

The meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order by Joseph Leigeb, chairperson at 7:00 p.m.

Roll call was taken: Joseph Leigeb, Sheila Mesler, Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza were present.

Minutes of the July 1, 2003 meeting were read. Motion by Gary Elza support by Denise Schneider to accept the minutes as read. Roll call vote, passed 5-0. A comment from Kevin Wray that both section 308 and 1303 D as read in the minutes were presented to the Township Board and accepted as written.

Motion by Denise Schneider support by Sheila Mesler to accept the agenda with two additions as presented. Approved.

Chairperson Leigeb presented the site plan review to the commission and welcomed Mr. John Baer from Pumford Construction Inc. to answer questions. Mr. Baer was representing the property owner on the proposed use of the subject property for storage units. After discussion and questions no action was taken at this time as Mr. Baer is going to do additional work on the site plan and return at a later date if they decide to move forward.

Chairperson Leigeb moved to the Open Discussion portion of the meeting for Commission members and public input. The question was asked why should someone have to come to a Planning Commission meeting to get information that should be available to the public on an as needed basis. Should the Township ordinance book be on the web site? The long-range plan is yes, but it is not known when that will happen. Current copies of the book need to be made available to the Commission and the public when requested. There is also a proposal in place and being looked at to update the entire ordinance book.

There was no old business presented.

Under new business there were three items on the agenda.

It was questioned if the Township should have something pertaining to Temporary Carports & Other Type Canopy’s. At this time no action was taken, just discussion as to the need for it.

A sample copy of a Mineral Mining Ordinance was included in the packet for review by the Commission to be talked about more at future meetings.

Janet Asphalt made a request to modify their previous site plan with the following change. Replace the office structure with a 24’ X 40’ double office trailer with skirting; reduce the pole barn building to a 40’ X 40’ structure with a 20’ space between the office and this building. The rest of the site plan will remain the same adhering to all existing ordinance and building codes. Motion by Sheila Mesler support by Denise Schneider to amend the prior site plan for Janet Asphalt to install a 24’ X 40’ double office trailer with skirting, reduce the pole barn to a 40’ X 40’ structure set 20’ from the office structure. The rest of the site plan as approved before will remain the same. Roll call vote approved 5-0.

The next meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission is scheduled for September 16, 2003 at 7:00 p.m. in the township hall.

Meeting was adjourned at 8:10 p.m.

 

 

Gary Elza, Secretary

Lincoln Township Planning Commission

Midland, Michigan
