LINCOLN TOWNSHIP-MIDLAND COUNTY

Planning Commission

 

June 1, 2004

Joseph Leigeb – Chairperson, Sheila Mesler – Vice-Chairperson

Members: Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza

The meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order by Joseph Leigeb, chairperson at 6:30 p.m.

Roll call was taken: Joseph Leigeb, Sheila Mesler, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza were present. Denise Schneider was absent

Motion by Kevin Wray with support by Gary Elza to accept the minutes of the May 4, 2004 meeting. One correction to be made in the seventh paragraph, Sheila is misspelled. Motion approved.

Motion by Gary Elza with support by Kevin Wray to accept the agenda as presented. Motion approved.

The site plan review as submitted for Sanford Med. Center PC was discussed. Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Gary Elza to accept the site plan as submitted. Motion approved.

The site plan review as submitted for Hang Time Hoops was discussed. Keith Thurlow and Jerry Schafer answered question regarding the site plan. Motion by Kevin Wray with support by Sheila Mesler to accept the site plan as submitted. Motion approved.

At this time the planning commission continued review of the zoning text revisions that have been submitted by The Spicer Group. Additional questions will be sent back to them for clarification. At this point the next meeting to work on these changes was set for June 22, 2004 at 6:30 p.m. in the Lincoln Township hall.

Chairperson Leigeb requested comments from the commission members and the public. There were none presented.

Under old business it was discussed as to the procedure regarding site plan reviews that had been approved but there was no action being taken on them by the applicants. What or who is to take action in regard to these? It was reviewed that the building inspector would be the person to address this as it falls into his jurisdiction. This may need clarification from the township board at a later date.

Chairperson Leigeb requested new business and Gene Frank requested clarification as to what zoning his property at 66 E. Price Rd. was. It was determined that it was in B-2. It was discussed how the 100’ set back applied to B-2 zoning when the adjoining property is R-1 or R-2. It was suggested the Mr. Frank review his property and the possible uses of it and or the possible rezoning that may work better for him, these were options that he needs to investigate.

No additional business was presented.

The next meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning commission is scheduled for June 22, 2004 at 6:30 p.m. in the township hall.

Chairperson Leigeb adjourned the meeting at 8:06 p.m.

Gary Elza, Secretary

Lincoln Township Planning Commission

Midland County
