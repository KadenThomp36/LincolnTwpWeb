LINCOLN TOWNSHIP-MIDLAND COUNTY

Planning Commission

 

August 24, 2004

Joseph Leigeb – Chairperson, Sheila Mesler – Vice-Chairperson

Members: Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza

 

August 24, 2004

The meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order by Joseph Leigeb, chairperson at 6:30 p.m.

Roll call was taken: Joseph Leigeb, Sheila Mesler, Kevin Wray, Gary Elza and Denise Schneider were present.

Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Kevin Wray to accept the minutes of the July 6, 2004 meeting. One correction, in the 5th paragraph an "s" needs to be added to Rapano in the second line. Motion approved.

Motion by Gary Elza with support by Denise Schneider to accept the agenda as presented. Motion approved.

The commission reviewed the revised zoning text; minor corrections were noted in a clean copy of the text. This will be sent back to Cindy Winland of The Spicer Group for corrections and then chairperson Leigeb will schedule a public hearing for the new zoning text revisions.

Chairperson Leigeb went to the open discussion portion of the meeting, there was nothing presented from the commission or the public at this time.

There was no old business presented to the commission at this time.

There was no new business presented to the commission at this time.

The next scheduled meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission is scheduled for September 21, 2004 at 6:30 p.m. in the Lincoln Township hall.

Chairperson Leigeb adjourned the meeting at 7:10 p.m.

Gary Elza, Secretary

Lincoln Township Planning Commission
