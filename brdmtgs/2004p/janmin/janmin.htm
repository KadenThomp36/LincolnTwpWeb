LINCOLN TOWNSHIP-MIDLAND COUNTY

Planning Commission

 

JANUARY 6, 2004

Joseph Leigeb – Chairperson, Sheila Mesler – Vice-Chairperson

Members: Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza

January 6, 2004

The meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order by Joseph Leigeb, chairperson at 7:00 p.m.

Roll call was taken: Joseph Leigeb, Sheila Mesler, Denise Schneider, Kevin Wray and Gary Elza were present.

Motion by Kevin Wray with support by Denise Schneider to accept the minutes of the December 2, 2003 meeting. Approved 5 – 0.

Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Denise Schneider to accept the agenda with the addition of item 3a: Election of Officer. Approved 5 –0.

Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Denise Schneider for Joseph Leigeb to be Chairperson for the year 2004. Motion approved 5 – 0.

Motion by Denise Schneider with support by Gary Elza for Sheila Mesler to be Vice-Chairperson for the year 2004. Motion approved 5 – 0.

Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Denise Schneider for Gary Elza to be Secretary for the year 2004. Motion approved 5 – 0.

Cindy Winland from the Spicer Group made a presentation regarding the zoning text improvements and up-date. The planning commission went through a hand out that Cindy presented as to possible changes. Cindy also took back a list of items discussed for consideration. At the March meeting the commission will review the changes discussed tonight. Cindy will mail a copy to each commission member along with Jerold Bailey and Henry Pnacek for review before the meeting.

Chairperson Leigeb requested any comments from the public on any issue. Jerold Bailey requested information regarding a building on Virgina Drive. It was handled at the Township Board level and had been finalized as per Kevin Wray.

There were no additional comments for commission members.

There was no old business presented at this time.

There was no new business presented at this time.

The next meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission is scheduled for March 2, 2004 at 7:00 p.m. in the township hall.

Chairperson Leigeb adjourned the meeting at 8:20 p.m.

Gary Elza, Secretary

Lincoln Township Planning Commission

Midland County, Michigan
