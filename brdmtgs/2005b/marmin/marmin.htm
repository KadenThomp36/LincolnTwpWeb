LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

 MARCH 8, 2005

  

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor with all members present, except Ensign, and 17 guests. The agenda was amended by motion from Hughes. Public comment was heard.

The minutes of February's meeting were approved by motion from Oswald. Approved unanimously. The treasurer's report was given.

Motion to pay bills by Oswald. Roll call vote: Briggs-YES, Wray-YES, Oswald-YES, Hughes-YES. Approved unanimously.

Motion to hire new fire fighter by Hughes. Roll call vote: Hughes-YES, Oswald-YES, Wray-YES, Briggs-YES. Approved unanimously.

The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement officer, and water district reports were given.

Motion to begin variance for old hall property by Hughes. Approved unanimously.

Motion to accept 2005 road program with road commission by Hughes. Roll call vote; Oswald-YES, Wray ?YES, Briggs-YES, Hughes-YES. Approved unanimously.

Motion to approve anniversary date change with Municipal Retirement Systems by Hughes .Approved unanimously..

Resolution approved for amending articles of incorporation for water district. By motion from Hughes. Approved unanimously.

Motion to set May 6-7 & 13-14 as free trash weekends for residents by Oswald. Approved unanimously.

Meeting adjourned at 8:35 pm.

 

Larry Hughes, Clerk
