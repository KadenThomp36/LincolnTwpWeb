LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

AUGUST 12, 2003

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the supervisor with all members present and 15 guests.
The pledge of allegiance was given and public comment was heard. A petition from Steven Klass was received by residents of Lincoln Estates requesting road repairs.
Sheets motioned with Ahearn seconding to accept minutes of July meeting . Motion approved.
The treasurers report was given.
Wray motioned with Ahearn seconding to approve payment of bills as presented. Roll call vote: Wray-YES, Nightlinger-YES, Ahearn-YES, Hughes-YES, Sheets-YES. Approved unanimously.
The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement, and water district reports were given.
Hughes motioned with Ahearn seconding to table request by developer of Stargazer Condominium Site for township agreement for joint septic system. Motion approved.
Wray motioned with Hughes seconding Spicer Group proposal for revisions of zoning ordinances including mining ordinance at cost of $6200.00.. Roll call vote: Sheets-NO, Hughes-YES, Ahearn-YES, Nightlinger-YES, Wray-YES. Motion approved.
Wray motioned with Sheets seconding request from John Rika for street light on Pinesboro. Roll call vote: Ahearn-NO, Wray-NO, Sheets-NO, Nightlinger-NO, Hughes-NO. Motion failed unanimously.
Hughes motioned with Sheets seconding repair of four sections of fence at township hall. Roll call vote: Hughes-YES, Wray-YES, Sheets-YES, Ahearn-YES, Nightlinger-YES. Approved unanimously.
Meeting adjourned. 8:06 PM


Larry Hughes Clerk
