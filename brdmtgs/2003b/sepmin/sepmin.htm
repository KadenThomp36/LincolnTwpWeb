LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

SEPTEMBER 9, 2003
The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the supervisor with all members present and 11 guests. The pledge was given and the agenda was accepted by motion from Wray and seconded by Sheets.  

The minutes of August's meeting were approved by motion from Sheets and seconded by Ahearn. 

The treasurer's report was given.

Ahearn motioned with Sheets seconding payment of bills. Roll call vote: Wray-YES, Nightlinger-YES, Ahearn-YES, Hughes-YES, Sheets-YES. Approved unanimously.

The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement, and water district reports were given.

Stargazer Condominium Site request was tabled until October by motion from Hughes with second by Ahearn.

Meeting adjourned 7:26 pm



Larry Hughes Clerk
