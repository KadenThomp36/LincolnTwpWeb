LINCOLN TOWNSHIP-MIDLAND COUNTY

Planning Commission

 

December 17, 2007

 

Susan Ensign-Chairperson, Sheila Mesler-Vice Chairperson

Denise Schneider-Acting Secretary

Members: Roger Crockett, Reid Duford

 

 

The meeting of the Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order by Chairperson Susan Ensign at 6:40 pm.

 

Roll call was taken:  Susan Ensign, Sheila Mesler, Roger Crockett & Denise Schneider were present, Reid Duford was absent.

 

Motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Roger Crockett to accept the agenda as presented.  Motion approved 4-0.

 

 Motion by Roger Crockett with support by Sheila Mesler to approve the minutes of the October 2, 2007 meeting. Motion approved 4-0.

 

Susan Ensign-Chairperson recessed the regular meeting  to address the Site Plan Review for Billboards by Johnson.

 

Mr. Johnson presented his requests. Many questions to Mr. Johnson about the plan from the planning commission.  Sheila Mesler motioned to accept the Site plan with the lighting to be mounted on bottom of the face of the billboard.  Roger Crockett supported it.  Motion approved 4-0.

 

Susan Ensign-chairperson called the regular meeting back in session.

 

Old Business was discussion on the review of Master Plan by Rowe,Inc

which was sent to the Township Board and they have advised to wait until March before any action will be taken. More discussion by the planning Commission about the growth of Midland area moving to our area. 

 

Next order of business was Election of Officers for the Planning Commission for 2008.  Sheila Mesler nominated Sue Ensign for Chairperson supported by Denise Schneider. Approved 4-0.  Denise Schneider nominated Sheila Mesler for Vice-Chairperson supported by Roger Crockett. Approved 4-0.  Sheila Mesler nominated Reid Duford for Secretary supported by Denise Schneider.  Approved 4-0.

 

Resolution for approval of the 2008’ Planning Commission Meetings.

 

January 8, 2008

March 4, 

May 6

July 1

September 2

November 4

Roll call was taken, approved 4-0.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 7:30 pm.

 

Denise Schneider, Acting Secretary
