LINCOLN TOWNSHIP-MIDLAND COUNTY

Planning Commission

 

October 2, 2007

 

Susan Ensign-Chairperson, Sheila Mesler-Vice-Chairperson

Gary Elza- Secretary

Members: Denise Schneider,Roger Crockett

 

 

The meeting of Lincoln Township Planning Commission was called to order By Susan Ensign, Chairperson at 6:37pm.

 

Roll call was taken:  Susan Ensign, Sheila Mesler,  Denise Schneider and Roger Crockett were present Gary Elza was absent.

 

A motion by Denise Schneider with support by Sheila Mesler to accept the agenda as presented. Motion approved 4 – 0.

 

A motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Roger Crockett to accept the minutes of the July 24, 2007  meeting. Motion approved 4 – 0.

 

Comments by the Planning Commission members as to the resignation of Gary Elza  as of December 31, 2007.

 

No public Comments.

 

No Old Business

 

New Business:  Justin Sprague from Rowe Inc. was present with proposal to Review the Lincoln Township’s Master Plan.  It is recommended that the Master Plan be reviewed about every 5 years.

 

A motion by Sheila Mesler with support by Roger Crockett to Recommend Approval of the Review of Master Plan by Rowe Inc. to the Township Board.  Upon Approval of the Township Board, Rowe Inc. will e-mail a copy of the Review and updates to the Planning Commissions Members.

Motion Approved  4- 0 .

 

Chairperson Ensign adjourned the meeting at 7:00pm.

 

 

Denise Schneider, Acting Secretary

Lincoln Township Planning Commission

Midland County
