LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

 JUNE 8, 2004

 

  

LINCOLN TOWNSHIP REGULAR BOARD MEETING 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor with all members present and 16 guests. The pledge was given and the agenda was amended to include hall parking lot.

Public comment was heard and correspondence was read regarding 911, County Connection, and Mosquito Control upcoming millage request. Hughes motioned and Wray seconded Clerks attendance of MTA seminar on July 27 for required booking procedures.

The minutes of May’s meeting were approved by motion from Wray and seconded by Ahearn.

The treasurer’s report was given.

A motion to pay bills on hand was made by Sheets and seconded by Ahearn. Roll call vote: Wray- YES, Nightlinger-YES, Ahearn-YES, Hughes-YES, Sheets- YES. Approved unanimously.

The fire department, planning commission, building inspectors, code enforcement, and water district reports were heard.

Under old business the Stark Road water line was discussed.

An update of the free trash weekend was given.

A motion by Hughes and second by Sheets was approved to seek bids on demolition of old hall and an obtain appraisal for property.

Meeting adjourned at 7:55 pm.

 

Larry Hughes, Clerk
