LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

 OCTOBER 12, 2004

 

  


  The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the supervisor with all board members present and 26 guests.
  The agenda was amended by motion from Sheets and seconded by Ahearn.
  Public comment was heard and correspondence was read.
  Motion by Hughes and seconded by Ahearn to allow new treasurer and deputy to attend MTA seminar for treasurers in November. Roll call vote: Wray-YES, Nightlinger-YES, Ahearn-YES, Hughes-YES Sheets-YES. Approved unanimously.
  Public hearing set for November meeting for water resolution by motion from Hughes.
  The minutes of September's meeting approved by motion from Sheets and seconded by Ahearn.
  The treasurer's report was given.
  Motion to pay bills on hand by Ahearn and seconded by Wray. Roll call vote: Sheets-YES Hughes-YES, Ahearn-YES, Nightlinger-YES, Wray-YES. Approved unanimously.
  The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement officer, and water district reports were heard.
  Meeting date set with Midland County Road Commission on November 4 for 2005 road work.
  Attendance and associated cost of annual MTA conference in Detroit in January by board members and deputies approved by motion from Hughes and seconded by Sheets.
  Motion to accept private road maintenance agreement with road commission for Pine Ridge
by Nightlinger and seconded by Hughes. Roll call vote: Sheets-YES, Wray-YES, Ahearn-YES, Hughes-YES, Nightlinger- YES. Approved unanimously.
  Internet access installation for township hall approved by motion from Nightlinger and seconded by Wray.  
  Meeting adjourned at 7:43 pm.
 


  Larry Hughes, Clerk
