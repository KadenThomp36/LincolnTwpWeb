LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

September 8, 2009

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, with 16 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Oswald and second by Crockett.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the August 11, 2009 regular board meeting was approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurerï¿½s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $15,232.21 in the general fund, $1,663.40 in the fire fund, and $157.59 in the liquor fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Crockett and second by Ensign.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Discussed and set tentative date for meeting with the Midland County Road Commission to discuss the 2010 road program.

 

Received paperwork for IFT request for 1550 E. Virginia Drive and will consider request at October 13, 2009 board meeting.

 

Discussed consideration of adding conditional zoning to the Lincoln Township Zoning Ordinance.  No action was taken.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:21 pm by Supervisor Wray.
