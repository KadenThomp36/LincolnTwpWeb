LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

January 13, 2009

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, with 16 guests.

 

The agenda was approved with addition of appointments of water district #1 representatives after motion by Oswald and second by Crockett.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the December 9, 2008 regular board meeting were approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurerï¿½s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $24,515.18 in the general fund, $1,358.21 in the fire fund, $157.59 in the liquor fund, and $4,742.51 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Crockett and second by Oswald.  Wray-yes; Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Approved meeting dates for 2009 after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.  Wray-yes; Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes.

 

Approved reappointment of Sheila Mesler to planning commission with term expiring 12/31/11 after motion by Wray and second by Crockett.  Approved reappointment of Reid Duford to zoning board of appeals with term expiring 12/31/11 after motion by Wray and second by Crockett.  Approved reappointment of Steve Market to code enforcement officer with term expiring 12/31/09 after motion by Wray and second by Ensign.

 

Approved attendance at the MTA board of review training for those wishing to attend after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

Approved attendance at the MTA budget building seminar for those wishing to attend after motion by Crockett and second by Oswald.

 

Approved language changes to Township pension plan after motion by Oswald and second by Trinklein.

 

Approved reappointment of Kevin Wray and Kim Oswald as water district #1 representatives after motion by Crockett and second by Ensign.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:01 pm by Supervisor Wray.
