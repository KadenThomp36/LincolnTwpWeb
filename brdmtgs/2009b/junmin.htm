LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

June 9, 2009

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, with 17 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the May 12, 2009 regular board meeting was approved after motion by Crockett and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurer’s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $14,797.05 in the general fund, $2,325.50 in the fire fund, $157.59 in the liquor fund, and $15,155.66 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Ensign and second by Crockett.  Ensign-yes; Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Set July 11, 2009 from 8am to 3pm as scrap tire collection day at township hall.

 

Approved attendance at MTA seminar on Smart Strategies for Dealing with Undesirable Land after motion by Oswald and second by Crockett.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 7:55 pm by Supervisor Wray.
