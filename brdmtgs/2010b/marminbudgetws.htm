LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD

BUDGET WORKSHOP MEETING MINUTES

March 16, 2010

  

The budget workshop meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the supervisor at 6:00 pm with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett and Trinklein present, and 1 guest.

 

Discussion was held on the 2010/2011 fiscal year budget of Lincoln Township.  A proposed budget was developed.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 7:46 pm by supervisor Wray.
