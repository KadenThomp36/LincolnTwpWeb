LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

February 9, 2010

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, and 15 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the January 12, 2010 regular board meeting was approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurer’s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $11,816.48 in the general fund, $1,878.94 in the fire fund, 157.59 in the liquor fund, and $7,807.71 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Crockett and second by Ensign.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and solid waste reports were heard.

 

Approved resolution to grant petition for property located at 1321 East Wackerly Road (Property code 110-035-400-070-00) to be annexed to the city of Midland after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

Reappointed Velsor Richardson, Denise Schneider, and Delbert Miller to the Board of Review with terms expiring 12/31/12 after motion by Trinklein and second by Wray.

 

Appointed Dana Maier as an alternate to the Board of Review with term expiring 12/31/12 after motion by Wray and second by Trinklein.

 

Approved setting budget workshop date for 3/16/10 at 6:00pm after motion by Trinklein and second by Oswald. 

 

Approved setting budget hearing/truth in taxation date for 3/23/10 at 7:00pm after motion by Trinklein and second by Ensign.

 

Resolution for no increase in supervisor, clerk, treasurer, and trustee salaries was approved by roll call vote after motion by Oswald and second by Crockett.  Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:17 pm by Supervisor Wray.
