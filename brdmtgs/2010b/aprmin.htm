LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

April 13, 2010

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, and 16 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Oswald and second by Crockett.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the March 9, 2010 regular board meeting, March 16, 2010 budget workshop, and March 23, 2010 special meeting were approved after motion by Ensign and second by Crockett.

 

The treasurerï¿½s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $25,289.71 in the general fund, $1,079.14 in the fire fund, 157.59 in the liquor fund, $19,576.25 in the water fund, and $8,021.79 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Crockett and second by Oswald.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and solid waste reports were heard.

 

Approved attendance at various MTA seminars after motion by Ensign and second by Crockett.

 

Approved by roll call vote Township insurance package proposal from Trident Insurance through Molthourp Clift Insurance after motion by Trinklein and second by Ensign.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

Accepted resignation of Denise Schneider from the planning commission and board of review after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:52 pm by Supervisor Wray.
