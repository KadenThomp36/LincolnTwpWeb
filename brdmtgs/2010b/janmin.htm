LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

January 12, 2010

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, and 16 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Crockett and second by Ensign.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the December 9, 2009 regular board meeting was approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurerï¿½s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $35,821.88 in the general fund, $2,237.90 in the fire fund, 446.79 in the liquor fund, and $11,526.80 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Crockett and second by Ensign.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Reappointed Reid Duford to the Planning Commission with term expiring 12/31/12 after motion by Wray and second by Oswald.

 

Reappointed Monty McCann to the Zoning Board of Appeals with term expiring 12/31/12 after motion by Wray and second by Ensign.

 

Reappointed Steve Marker to Ordinance Enforcement Officer with term expiring 12/31/10 after motion by Wray and second by Ensign.

 

Appointed Chris Clark to Planning Commission with term expiring 12/31/11 after motion by Wray and second by Oswald.

 

Approved entering into fire truck financing agreement by roll call vote after motion by Trinklein and second by Crockett.  Crockett-yes; Oswald-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes; Wray-yes.

 

Approved attendance at a MTA education seminar after motion by Trinklein and second by Ensign.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:17 pm by Supervisor Wray.
