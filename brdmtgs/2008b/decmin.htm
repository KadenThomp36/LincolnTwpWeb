LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

December 9, 2008

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:02 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Crockett, and Trinklein present, with 14 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the November 11, 2008 regular board meeting were approved after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

The treasurer’s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills in the amount of $14,411.99 in the general fund, $1,894.69 in the fire fund, $157.59 in the liquor fund, and $7,773.85 in the sanitation fund were approved by roll call vote after motion by Ensign and second by Crockett.  Wray-yes; Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes.

 

The fire department, planning committee, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Approved deficit elimination plan for the water fund after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.  Wray-yes; Oswald-yes; Crockett-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes.

 

Approved snow plowing contract with Lyle Jacobs after motion by Wray and second by Oswald.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 7:54 pm by Supervisor Wray.
