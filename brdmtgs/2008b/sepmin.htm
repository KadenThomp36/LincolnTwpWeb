LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING MINUTES

 September 9, 2008

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor at 7:00 pm, with Wray, Oswald, Ensign, Surma, and Trinklein present, with 20 guests.

 

The agenda was approved after motion by Surma and second by Oswald.

 

Public comments were heard and correspondence was read.

 

The minutes of the August 12, 2008 regular board meeting were approved after motion by Ensign and second by Oswald.

 

The treasurer’s report was heard.

 

Payment of bills were approved by roll call vote after motion by Trinklein and second by Ensign.  Wray-yes; Oswald-yes; Surma-yes; Ensign-yes; Trinklein-yes.

 

The fire department, building inspector, ordinance enforcement officer, water authority, and sold waste reports were heard.

 

Discussed meeting times with Midland County Road Commission regarding 2009 road projects.

 

Approved Michigan Township Association seminar for those on planning commission wishing to attend at a cost of $70 each after motion by Oswald and second by Ensign.

 

Approved electrical inspector to take the electrical inspectors update class at a cost of $590 after motion by Ensign and second by Trinklein.

 

Discussed Water District #1 project contract.  Item will be discussed further at October board meeting.

 

Meeting was adjourned at 8:12 pm by Supervisor Wray.
