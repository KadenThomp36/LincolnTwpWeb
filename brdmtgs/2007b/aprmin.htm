LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

April 10, 2007
 

Regular meeting called to order by Supervisor with all present and 17 guests.

 

Agenda amended to include refrigerator for hall after motion by Oswald.

 

Correspondence was read and included Mosquito control, Conservation district meetings,

Stark Road water testing, Solid waste advisory committee, and burning permits.

 

Minutes of March 13, regular meeting approved after motion by Oswald.

 

Minutes of March 20, budget workshop meeting approved after motion by Ensign.

 

Minutes of March 22, adoption of budget hearing approved after motion by Oswald.

 

The Treasurer’s report was given.

 

Motion to pay bills on hand by Hughes. Roll call vote: Briggs - YES, Wray - YES, Oswald - YES, Hughes - YES, Ensign - YES. Approved Unanimously.

 

The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement, and water district reports were given.

 

Approved hiring of Jerry Schcosser as fire fighter trainee after motion by Hughes.

 

Scott Walker from Midland Tomorrow spoke.

 

Approved attendance of M.C.T.O.A. meeting on June 7, after motion by Hughes.

 

Approved attendance of M.T.A. Spring meeting on May 24, after motion by Oswald.

 

Approved purchase of signs for township property after motion by Ensign.

 

Approved purchase of refrigerator for hall after motion by Hughes.

 

Meeting adjourned at 8:25 p.m.

 Larry Hughes, Clerk
