LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

September 11, 2007

 

 

The regular meeting of the board was called to order by the Supervisor with all present

and 17 guests.

 

The agenda was accepted after motion by Ensign.

 

Public comments were heard regarding hall painting, snow plowing, fire hydrant restrictions, and solid waste pickup.

 

Correspondence was read including F.E.M.A. Flood mapping, liquor control letter, tire recycling, house bills 4780 through 4788, and M.T.A. seminars.

 

The minutes of August meeting were approved after motion by Oswald.

 

The treasurer’s report was given.

 

Motion to pay bills on hand by Ensign. Roll call vote: Briggs - YES, Wray - YES, Oswald - YES, Hughes - YES, Ensign - YES. Approved unanimously.

 

The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement officer, and water district reports were given.

 

Meeting with road commission set for September 20 at 9:45a.m.

 

Water district service agreement approved after motion by Wray.

 

Set curb side pickup on Tuesdays with free containers to residents after motion by Hughes.

 

 

Meeting adjourned at 8:25 p.m.

Larry Hughes, Clerk
