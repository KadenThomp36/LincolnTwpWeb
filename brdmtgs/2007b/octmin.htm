LINCOLN TOWNSHIP BOARD MEETING

October 9, 2007

 

 

The regular meeting of the Lincoln Township Board was called to order by the Supervisor with all present and 18 guests.

 

The agenda was accepted after motion by Oswald.

 

Public comment was heard regarding Saginaw Road tree cutting and Price Road paving.

 

County Commissioner Bill Waterman spoke.

 

Correspondence was read.

 

Minutes of September meeting were approved after motion by Ensign.

 

The treasurerï¿½s report was given. Motion to pay bills by Oswald. Roll call vote: Briggs-YES, Wray-YES, Oswald-YES, Hughes-YES, Ensign-YES. Approved unanimously.

 

The fire department, planning commission, building inspector, code enforcement officer, and water district reports were given.

 

Approved budget amendment to move $1800.00 from general fund to elections after motion by Hughes.

 

Approved resignation of Ensign from Z.B.A. after motion by Oswald.

 

Approved resignation of Elza from planning commission after motion by Hughes.

 

Approved appointment of Reid Duford to planning commission after motion by Wray.

 

Approved appointment of Dana Mier to Z.B.A. after motion by Wray.

 

Approved attendance of M.T.A. conference in January after motion by Oswald.

 

Discussed 2008 road projects.

 

Meeting adjourned at 8:05 p.m.

 

Larry Hughes, Clerk
